Who I Am

I'm a sophomore Cybersecurity student at the University of South Florida with a background in Electrical Engineering. My core lane is Digital Forensics and Incident Response (DFIR) and threat intelligence, and I'm expanding into signals security and hardware/firmware. I bring an engineering foundation to security work, which means I understand the physical layer most analysts never touch.

Everything right now is infrastructure. The long-term target is founding the Tactical Intelligence Group (TIG): a defense technology firm operating at the physical-digital threat boundary. Elite DFIR internship Summer 2027. That's the mission.
