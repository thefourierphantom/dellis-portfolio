01 Operational Environment

THE SIGNAL IS
THE FIRST CASUALTY.

Every modern platform assumes two things: it knows where it is, and it can talk to friendly systems. In a contested environment, both assumptions are attacked directly — GPS jamming and spoofing deny trusted position; RF jamming severs coordination and data links.

The result is a capability gap that senior defense and intelligence leaders return to constantly: systems that perform flawlessly in the lab become blind and mute exactly when the mission depends on them.

Try it yourself. Raise the jamming power and watch the link degrade — then note what has to happen next. That "next" is where Ellis Dynamics operates.
