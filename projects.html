ELLISDYNAMICS

I'm building Ellis Dynamics — a countermeasure systems startup protecting friendly platforms from electronic denial. Newly launched Summer 2026. When GPS is jammed or spoofed and radio links are suppressed, platforms lose position and coordination at the exact moment the mission depends on them. Ellis Dynamics builds hardware for that moment: an inertial dead-reckoning navigation module that maintains position awareness through GPS denial, and a predictive tracking and precision-pointing system that detects aerial threats and keeps sensors locked on them — the tracking layer every counter-UAS defeat method depends on.

Sensor & Actuator Integration

Physically integrating IMU, EO/IR, and future RF hardware onto a shared platform.

PIL·03

Precision Pointing / Gimbal Control

Two-axis gimbal driven by predictive feedforward from the tracker's own state estimate — leading a moving target instead of lagging it. Where Detect-Track and robotics become one system.

Size, weight, and power discipline for small-platform deployability — the practical filter most small-platform defense proposals are screened against.
